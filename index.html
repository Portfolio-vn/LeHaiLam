Why Engineering?

My work starts with structure, then becomes a system.

I approach engineering as a disciplined creative process. A strong idea is not enough; it must be translated into dimensions, wiring, firmware, testing, and evidence.

Through robotics and independent hardware projects, I learned to balance three things: mechanical reliability, electronic clarity, and a user interface that makes the system easy to understand.
